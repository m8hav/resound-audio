Currently a work in progress, this new music streaming platform would allow users to upload their own music and share it for the world to listen. Besides all the features a regular music streaming website has, users also get an inbuilt messenger so they never have to leave the platform to share a song with their friends. Moreover, it's an interactive platform, meaning users find a comment section under every song and can discuss everything they like about the song.

It's the new Social Media of Music Producers...
